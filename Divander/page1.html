What is Divander Studios?

Here at Divander Studios we stand for 3 things: Community, Innovaton, and Teamwork. Divander is a portmanteau of Diversity and Anders - the German word for different. As well, with Virtual Reality, the player dives in, hence our logo resembling a tunnel. The colors that make up our company are orange - for social interaction, purple - for awareness, and gray - relaibility, neutrality, and protection. This is what we want people to feel when they think of Divander Studios and we want to show that in our products. Together, these are the aspects the come to make us Divander Studios
