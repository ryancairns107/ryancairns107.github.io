Myths about 'The Bully'

  • Children who bully are loners
  • Children who bully have low self-esteem

Characteristics of 'The Bully'

  • Have a need to feel powerful and in control
  • Gain satisfaction from inflicting suffering on others
  • Display little empathy and compassion for others
  • Defend their actions by blaming the victim
  • Are generally defiant towards adults
  • Have strong self-esteem
