How it works?
-

Index | The Screen | The System


The built in barcode scanner first scans all items that go in and out of the fridge in real-time. Along with this, a camera is used that uses a machine learning system to begin to recognize all products that go in and out of the fridge. Once the AI has many items in its database, it will begin to take over for all of the items entering and leaving the fridge. If the system is unsure of an item, it will be able to scan a barcode on an item or you can type what the item is on the screen on the fridge.
